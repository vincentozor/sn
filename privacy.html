Privacy Policy for ShediNet

Welcome to ShediNet! Protecting the privacy of our visitors is one of our highest priorities. This Privacy Policy explains the type of data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information. By visiting our website, https://ShediNet.com, you agree to the practices outlined below.

Need Assistance?

If you have any questions or need further clarification regarding this Privacy Policy, please don’t hesitate to contact us.

Understanding Log Files

Like most websites, ShediNet utilizes log files to record visitor activity. These files are standard practice among hosting services and help us analyze trends, manage the site, and gather general demographic data. Information collected includes:

  • IP addresses
  • Browser types
  • Internet Service Providers (ISPs)
  • Date and time stamps
  • Referring and exit pages
  • Click activity

Importantly, this data is not linked to personally identifiable information. It is purely used for statistical and operational purposes.

Cookies and Web Beacons

ShediNet employs cookies to enhance user experience. These cookies store data such as visitor preferences and the pages they access on our site. This allows us to customize content based on user behavior and browser type. For a deeper understanding of cookies, you can refer to resources like the “Cookies” article provided by the Privacy Policy Generator.

Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google, as a third-party vendor, uses DART cookies to display ads based on users’ visits to our website and other sites. However, users can opt-out of DART cookie usage by visiting Google’s ad and content network Privacy Policy at this link.

Advertising Partners

ShediNet collaborates with advertising partners who may use cookies and web beacons in their ad delivery. These technologies help them personalize ads and track campaign performance. Our key partner is:

  • Google

For more details, please review their individual Privacy Policies via the links provided.

Third-Party Data Practices

ShediNet’s Privacy Policy does not cover the practices of third-party advertisers or websites. We recommend reviewing their respective Privacy Policies to understand their data collection methods and opt-out options. You can also manage cookies through your browser settings for added control.

Children’s Privacy

Protecting children’s privacy is crucial. We encourage parents and guardians to supervise their children’s online activity. ShediNet does not intentionally collect personal information from individuals under the age of 13. If you believe your child has provided such data, please contact us immediately. We will promptly delete it from our records.

Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies strictly to data collected during online interactions with our website. It does not cover information gathered offline or through other channels.

Your Consent

By accessing and using our website, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms. We encourage you to read it thoroughly to understand your rights and our practices.
